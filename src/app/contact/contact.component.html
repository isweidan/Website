What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event in which programmers come together to collaborate on a project for a short period of time.

What Makes Our Hackathon Unique?

Our club, EPAI, consists of undergraduate students passionate about fostering critical discussions around the consequences of AI. Our hackathon will be unique due to its focus on the ethical aspects of AI, a pertinent topic that other hackathons don't typically cover.

Participation

We expect to have roughly 100 hackers participate in this event. Since the event will be virtual, we hope to attract students from across the globe to create a diverse and inclusive event.
